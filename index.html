From Your Sibling

I care about you deeply despite us going on our own paths in life.

It is said that as children we start as spirits in the void and choose a mother as a host, perhaps for karmic lessons we have to resolve in the material world. I think there is some significance in us choosing the same host for our mother.

I know that we've had to navigate our childhood with little guidance from our parents and therefore had to be our own parent. I intuitively realized that fact since I was younger and resolved myself to shield my inner self from the storms of life. That is the source of my constant hypervigilance and blunt speech devoid of consideration. For that, I apologize that I've similarly inflicted that upon you on occasions, it is still something I am reckoning with to this day even in my other relationships.

I always wanted to escape the ideal that people project onto me as a parental guide; because it's not me, as I am also similarly lost.

I want to bring to your awareness of the great capacity for people to think evil thoughts and leave a lot of it unspoken. How people who have blood on their hands can easily look at you with a smile and shake your hand; this I know too well as I have to mould myself into a similar being in order to survive in our profane world. Having to tread the paradox of coexistence and having to transact in the material realm. I don't ask that you take that point of view but from my lived experience it is much too apparent.

I apologize for being afraid of tenderness. It was always something I feared would bring me down and prevent me from surviving the evils of the world that I have to navigate.

I want you to use your own mind and make your own choices in life, scary as they may be.

When we were children, I always envied your affinity for memorizing numbers. That is why I offer this site—a gift of isopsephy and the analysis of your name.

With love, always.

Tyler

The Roots of Your Name

Etymology and historical meaning

Brianna

Celtic Origin

Root Meaning

Bre / Brig — "Hill, High, Noble, Exalted"

The Legacy of the High King

Your name is inextricable from Brian Boru (c. 941–1014), the legendary High King of Ireland who fundamentally altered history by breaking dynastic monopolies and shattering Viking power. This imbues your name with vibrations of revolution and unification—one who rises from humble origins to claim authority through merit, strategy, and strength.

The Goddess Brigid

The root brig connects you to Brigid, the pan-Celtic goddess of dawn, poetry, healing, and smithcraft. She represents the "Fire of the Head" (inspiration) and the "Fire of the Forge" (transformation). Your nobility is not just political rank but intellectual and creative elevation.

Poetry — Shaping reality through language Healing — Restoring wholeness to broken systems Smithcraft — Forging raw materials into beauty

Miranda

Latin Origin

Root Meaning

Mirandus — "To wonder at, to admire, to look at"

The Shakespearean Imprint

In The Tempest (1611), Shakespeare's Miranda is the daughter of the magician Prospero, raised in isolation on an enchanted island. She represents the Innocent Witness—one who sees potential, divinity, and beauty where others see only corruption.

"O brave new world, that has such people in't!"

The Mirror and the Miracle

The etymological link to mirror is profound. Mirrors reflect; they do not generate. You have an ability to show others their true nature, to act as a clear surface in a turbulent world. The connection to miracle implies you are a catalyst for the extraordinary.
